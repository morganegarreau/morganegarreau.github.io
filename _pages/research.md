<p style="color: #006400;"><u><b>Scientific interests</b></u></p>

<p style="font-size: 18px;">The goal of my research is to gain a better understanding in the role played by <b>hemodynamics</b> in the onset and development of cardiovascular pathologies. My activities revolve around two main research fields to quantify hemodynamics: <b>numerical simulations of blood flows (Computational Fluid Dynamics, CFD)</b> at the macroscopic scale and <b>4D flow MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging)</b>, a medical imaging technique which grants access to time-resolved three-dimensional blood velocity fields in a region of interest.</p>
